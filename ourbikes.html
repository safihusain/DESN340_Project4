In addition to fully refurbished bikes, we also sell two types of ‘as-is’ bikes:

Project Bikes have not been tuned up. They range from bare frames to complete bikes, but most will need additional parts or repairs before they can be safely used.

Quick Tuned Bikes are quickly repaired to be safe and functional. They are cheaper than our fully refurbished bikes, but are not fixed up to the same high standard and may need additional work to function optimally.
